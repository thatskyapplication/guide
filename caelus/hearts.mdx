## Gifting hearts

There are two ways to gift hearts. The first way is simple: `/heart gift`. The second way is to right-click any user and select "Gift Heart".

<img
  src="/images/gift_heart.webp"
  alt="Gift Heart"
  title="Gift Heart"
  style={{ width:"56%" }}
/>

<Info>
  Only **3 users** may be receive your heart gifts per day.
</Info>

## Double hearts

When double heart events occur in Sky, hearts gifted will be doubled with Caelus!

## History of hearts

You can see a history of all hearts gifted and received via `/heart history`.

<img
  src="/images/heart_history.webp"
  alt="Heart History"
  title="Heart History"
  style={{ width:"57%" }}
/>

After a good amount of hearts, the history will become paginated.
